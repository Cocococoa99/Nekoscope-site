Terms of Service

Last updated: May 29, 2026

These Terms of Service govern the use of NekoScope, a read-only creator analytics tool for authorized TikTok creators.

Purpose of NekoScope

NekoScope helps creators review their own authorized content performance data. It imports authorized public video metadata and engagement counts, stores local metric snapshots, and provides local analytics tools for content review.

Authorized use

  • Users may connect only TikTok accounts they own or are authorized to manage.
  • Users may use NekoScope to analyze their own authorized creator data.
  • Users are responsible for complying with TikTok's terms, developer policies, and applicable laws.

Read-only limitations

NekoScope does not use the Content Posting API and does not provide publishing tools. Users must not use NekoScope to upload, publish, edit, delete, like, comment, follow, message, scrape, or automate TikTok account actions.

TikTok Login Kit

NekoScope uses TikTok Login Kit so users can authorize read-only access to supported scopes, including user.info.basic, user.info.profile, user.info.stats, and video.list. Users can revoke authorization through TikTok settings or clear local tokens inside NekoScope.

Local data and backups

NekoScope stores analytics data locally on the user's device. Users are responsible for managing backups, exported files, and local database security.

Accuracy

Analytics results depend on platform APIs, user imports, screenshots, manual entries, and local calculations. NekoScope does not guarantee that every metric is available or complete. Missing API fields may be displayed as unavailable rather than zero.
